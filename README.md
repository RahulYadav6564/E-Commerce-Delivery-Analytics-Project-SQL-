E-Commerce Delivery Analytics Project (SQL)

##Overview
-This project analyzes e-commerce delivery data to understand platform performance, customer behavior, and operational efficiency.
-The goal is to extract actionable business insights using SQL for better decision-making.
-Business Problem
-E-commerce platforms face challenges like delivery delays, high refund rates, and inconsistent service quality.
-These issues impact customer satisfaction and revenue performance.

#Key Questions
-Which platform generates the highest revenue and order volume?
-How do delivery delays impact customer satisfaction?
-What is the refund rate and what does it indicate?
-Which product categories contribute the most revenue?
-How efficient are platforms in terms of delivery time?

#Dataset
-Source: Kaggle (E-Commerce Delivery Analytics Dataset)
-Size: ~200,000 records

#Key Features:
-Order ID
-Customer ID
-Platform (Swiggy Instamart, Blinkit, JioMart)
-Order Date & Time
-Delivery Time (minutes)
-Product Category
-Order Value (INR)
-Service Rating
-Delivery Delay
-Refund Requested

#Tools & Technologies
-SQL (MySQL) – Data querying and analysis
-MySQL Workbench – Query execution
-Excel – Data cleaning and preprocessing

#Key Analysis Performed
-Platform Performance Analysis
-Compared revenue and order volume across platforms
-Ranked platforms based on total revenue
-Delivery Efficiency Analysis
-Calculated average delivery time
-Identified platforms with higher delivery delays
-Customer Satisfaction Analysis
-Analyzed service ratings
-Compared ratings for delayed vs on-time deliveries

#Refund Analysis
-Calculated overall refund percentage
-Evaluated impact of delivery issues on refunds

#Product Category Analysis
-Identified top revenue-generating categories
-Analyzed category-wise order distribution

#Key Findings
-High Refund Rate (~45.8%) indicates potential issues in delivery or product quality
-Delivery delays reduce service ratings, impacting customer satisfaction
-Few product categories contribute majority of revenue, showing revenue concentration
-Platform performance varies significantly in terms of revenue and delivery efficiency

#Business Recommendations
-Improve delivery efficiency by optimizing logistics
-Reduce refund rate by addressing root causes
-Focus on high-performing product categories
-Optimize platform operations based on performance comparison

#Project Outcome
-Identified delivery performance and customer experience as key drivers of business success
-Provided insights to improve customer satisfaction and revenue performance
